Make your concern heard! Students being the future of our country, the State thrives to ensure that the students study with ease. The Student Grievance Redressal Portal has been developed with the vision to decrease the distance between the authorities and the students.This portal aims to speed up the process of addressing the concerns of the students.This portal is made available to the students of the Universities and Institutions for effective redressal and tracking of the status of their concerns.The State assures the students that each grievance will be effectively handled by the members of the Students Grievance Redressal Committee of their respective Institute.
